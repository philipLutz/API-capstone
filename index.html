What is Picturesque?

In an effort to bring some color to the monotony of checking a weather app that is usually an endless list of calculated predictions, Picturesque offers a simple, yet novel alternative to let you know what is going on outside and give you an exclusive visual array associated with that place to set the tone for your day. After searching a location, you will be able to see the weather and a color scheme generated using an original algorithm that incorporates both geographical and random elements to produce a unique RGB value.
